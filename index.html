Start young build often

Some say it’s “never too late” to start, but at Camp K12 we believe it’s never too early.

We chose to build our courses for KG – 12 students rather than colleges because frankly, college is too late.

Our goal is to work with young minds and to inculcate in them the healthy habits of problem solving and creativity.

We want students in Indian schools to develop a sense of confidence in their abilities to create and an addiction to innovation

We feel that it is this early exposure will allow our campers in India to be the makers, doers, and entrepreneurs of tomorrow.
